Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data collection

AverageHuman collects no personal data. All tests run entirely in your browser. No information is transmitted to any server.

Test scores are saved locally on your device so you can see your history across visits. This data never leaves your browser and can be cleared at any time through your browser's site data settings.

Third-party services

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts. Your browser makes a standard request to Google's CDN to download font files. No other third-party scripts or services are used.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will reflect it.
